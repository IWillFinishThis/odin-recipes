Macaroni and Cheese

Macaroni and cheese is adelicious side dish to entree.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni noodles
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Cheese of your choice
  • flour
  • milk
  • butter
  • salt

Steps

  1. Cook macaraoni noodles.
  2. Melt butter in a pot.
  3. Pour flower into the pot with butter and stir.
  4. Pour milk into the pot with butter and flour.
  5. Pour cheese and salt into the pot and stir.
  6. Pour noodles into the cheese sauce and mix.
