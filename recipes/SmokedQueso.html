Smoked Queso

Smoked queso gives the already incredible flavor of queso just a tad more flavor

Ingredients

  • Velveeta Cheese
  • Smoked Gooda
  • Chopped onions
  • Rotel
  • cooked breakfast sausage
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Set smoker to 300 degrees
  2. In a 9 x 13 foil pan, put in all ingredients.
  3. Place pan in smoker.
  4. Check and stir often
  5. Queso is done when all cheese is melted and combined with remaining ingredietns.
